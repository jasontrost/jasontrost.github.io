The Long Game

In 2008, I quit UBS to build a better betting exchange. Everyone said the incumbent was unbeatable.

Took 7 years before we turned the corner. Required what I call "extreme hubris with extreme work ethic."

Today: Smarkets processes billions in annual volume. 200,000+ customers. One of Europe's fastest-growing companies.

We've survived three prediction market hype cycles. This one feels different. Maybe.

I've learned more from 18 years of building from failure than success:

  • Market design matters more than marketing
  • Liquidity is everything (we run our own trading desk to market make it)
  • 2% commission beats 5% (we proved it against the incumbents)
  • Politics makes headlines. Sports makes money.
  • The US strategy? With hindsight, I screwed it up. Learning.

The current prediction market boom makes me both excited and nervous. We've been here before.
