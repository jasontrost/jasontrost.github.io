About Jason Trost

Short Bio

Jason Trost is the founder and CEO of Smarkets, one of the world's largest prediction markets. Founded in 2008, Smarkets processes billions in annual volume across 200,000+ customers. Prior to Smarkets, Jason worked in derivatives trading at UBS. He hosts "The Business of Betting" podcast and has been building prediction markets for 18 years.

Long Bio

Jason Trost founded Smarkets in 2008 after leaving UBS, where he worked in derivatives trading. Over 18 years, he's built Smarkets into one of Europe's largest betting exchanges, processing billions in annual volume with over 200,000 customers.

Smarkets was one of the first companies to challenge Betfair's dominance by offering lower commissions (2% vs 5%) and cleaner market design. The company survived seven years of growth challenges before breaking through, driven by what Trost calls "extreme hubris with extreme work ethic."

Today, Smarkets operates across the UK and Ireland with offices in London and Los Angeles. The platform has processed billions in volume and weathered multiple prediction market hype cycles.

Trost hosts "The Business of Betting" podcast, where he interviews founders and operators in the betting and prediction market space. He lives in London with his wife Astrid and their two children.
